## What you're building

An Antifragility Map is a personal strategic document. It maps where you are fragile, robust, or antifragile in relation to AI disruption — and shows you where to focus to convert fragility into strength.

This is the final exercise of the course. Do it carefully. The output has real value.

## The framework

For each of the three zones, you'll identify specifics about your current work and skills.

### Zone 1: Fragile — where AI threatens you

List the tasks, skills, or responsibilities in your work that:
- Are repetitive or formulaic
- Could be replaced by a good AI workflow
- Would leave you exposed if they disappeared

Be honest. This is not about catastrophising — it's about seeing clearly.

### Zone 2: Robust — where you're protected but not gaining

List the aspects of your work where:
- AI doesn't really threaten what you do
- But you're also not using AI to get stronger
- You're stable, but not improving your position

### Zone 3: Antifragile — where AI disruption helps you

List (or design) the aspects of your work where:
- More AI capability means more value for you
- Disruption to others creates opportunities you can step into
- Your irreplaceable skills combine with AI tools to produce something neither could alone

If this zone feels empty, that's the work. What would need to be true for you to move something from Zone 1 or 2 into Zone 3?

## The output document

Write a short document (even half a page) with three sections: Fragile, Robust, Antifragile. For each zone, list 2–4 specifics and one action you'll take in the next 30 days.

Save it. Return to it in three months.
